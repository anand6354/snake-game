How to Play

Objective

Guide your snake to eat other snakes and grow as large as possible!

Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move your snake
  • Space: Speed boost (limited)
  • P: Pause/Resume

Game Modes

  • Survival Mode: Last as long as possible
  • Time Attack: Get the biggest score in 60 seconds
  • Tournament: 3 rounds, best score wins

Power-ups

  • ⭐ Speed Boost: Temporary increased speed
  • 🛡️ Shield: Protection from one collision
  • ❄️ Freeze: Slow other snakes
  • 📉 Shrink: Temporarily reduce your size
